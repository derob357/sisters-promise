Denise's Journey

My journey began long before I was aware of my path. It started in the medical field. I worked with some very reputable doctors; family practitioners, dermatologist, trichologist and aestheticians. Some years later I made a switch and worked for a well renowned naturopath and acupuncture specialists.

I quickly learned and assimilated new skills and thoughts as it was very similar to the lifestyle and culture I grew up in. My Caribbean heritage had also consisted of memories of my mother and grandmother mixing and brewing teas, tinctures, and formulas from nature.

So it was no surprise that I gravitated towards Eastern Medicine and quickly learned how to make formulas and tinctures for myself, for clients and my family. Some years later by "Faith" (another well known and respectable Naturopathic teacher and practitioner) I was introduced to the idea of starting my own business.

"Fusing what I learned from both Eastern and Western medicine and making products, I had been making for years for myself and family, Sister's Promise was born."
